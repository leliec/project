Presentation Order Bias Analysis

Is there a fatigue effect?

The regression analysis shows a correlation of 0.024 with a p-value of 0.08. Since p > 0.05, we conclude that the order in which sounds were presented did not significantly bias the results. Yet we notice that less extreme high scores are given from the few respondents who answered more than 60 sounds.
