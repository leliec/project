Model

The analysis is based on a multivariate linear regression aimed at modeling the relationship between the perceived anxiety score based on synthesis parameters:

Yᵢ = β₀ + ∑ⱼ₌₁ᵖ βⱼ·Xᵢⱼ + εᵢ

Yᵢ: anxiety score of sound i
Xᵢⱼ: various synthesis parameters (ADSR envelope variables of the first oscillator) and added variables: the total number of oscillators used, the presence of each waveform (sinusoidal, triangular, square, sawtooth), the number of oscillators with a non-zero "fine" frequency, an indicator of a fundamental frequency deviation likely to generate dissonance or internal timbre movement.
βⱼ: estimated coefficients for each independent variable
εᵢ: error
